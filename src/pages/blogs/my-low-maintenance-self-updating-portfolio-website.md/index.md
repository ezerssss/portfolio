# My low maintenance self updating portfolio website

> I always forget to update my portfolio website, hopefully this does the job…

Date: July 17, 2026

## 2 years ago

That's the last time I updated my website, 2 years, two entire years. While I did not become a CEO of a successful company (9/10 startups fail and I'm at my 8th startup so 2 more to go! 😃), I have a decent amount of updates to my portfolio (internships, startups, projects, etc.) that's just laying there waiting to be forgotten.

## I'll always forget to update this site

Even if I somehow get the strength to update my website now, I know I'd never remember to update it again after 2 years haha. But I've already paid for a 3 year term for my domain and I don't want that to get wasted, thats like 20 bucks and I can already buy 10 [Jollibee](https://i.pinimg.com/236x/4e/e9/81/4ee9815c437fde2e90c6b4b19059637c.jpg) meals for that.

## GitHub is my hero

There's one site I always update though, and that's [GitHub](https://github.com/ezerssss). I have already built the habit of publishing my _cool and amazing_ projects to GitHub to not only let other people see my _work of art_, but also to allow me to delete it in my local machine for space saving purposes (and of course the whole working with others shebang that I don't get to utilize since nobody wants to contribute to my _state of the art_ projects).
![](Pasted%20image%2020260717183257.png)

## Merge them all

**Pun intended.** Anyways, I got the brilliant idea of just fetching my projects from GitHub (since that's really what I want to showcase either way) and display them in a neat way in my portfolio. The plan is to just link them to the README file of the project and BOOM! Self updating low maintenance portfolio BABYYYY.

#### But how about the work experience I hear you ask?

Don't care. JK. I haven't found a way to automate that just _yet_. I was thinking maybe have a mind reader installed in my cranium that polls my mind if I have an update but I think that's too much work. I eventually just settled on listing it on a YAML file I can easily update. I have some cool ideas already on how I can utilize that YAML file, like maybe some way to automatically generate a CV/Resume just from that file alone.

## Blogging cause why not? We're already here

I want to try out blogging since ever, and this is actually an attempt of my first ever blog.. and I'm already having a very hard time. It feels like my thoughts are so perfect in my head but the moment I touch the keyboard and see the letters pop up on my screen, all I see is shit ass content that I would not even bother to read. But that's okay, I would look at this in the future and vomit on how I used to write and compare it to how I'm writing and be proud of my self. It all starts with the first step is what I always say.
